Overview

Vegebox is a configurable platform for managing an automated garden. This project is currently in progress.
System components include:

  • Raspberry Pi and software
  • Pi HAT for interfacing with garden sensors and controls
  • Pi HAT for managing the system's solar power and batteries.

Features include:

  • Gather data such as soil moisture, temperature, and light levels in order to efficiently water plants.
  • Support for sensors such as temperature, light, moisture, and accessories
    such as cameras, solar panels, batteries, valves, and pumps
  • Remotely access the garden's data and configurations.
