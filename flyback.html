Overview

I am currently in a student research group working to design a device that will pasteurize low-moisture foods by radio-frequency heating. Last year, a prototype device was drafted by another group, but never finalized and built. Supervised by professor Arakaki, two students and I are working to improve the device design. Major system components of the pasteurization device include a flyback DC-DC converter, power MOSFET square wave inverter, and a food chamber. 

I am currently working on designing a 24V-300V flyback converter circuit and PCB. The flyback converter will be controlled by the LT3751 High Voltage Regulator Controller IC and monitored by an external microcontroller. The flyback converter PCB and this project portfolio entry are still in progress. The schematic is being finalized, and the next steps include PCB design, PCB manufacturing, and testing. This project portfolio will be updated as the project progresses. 

Software and Services:

  • Schematic and PCB Design: Altium Designer
  • Circuit Simulation: LTspice XVII
  • PCB Manufacturing: JLCPCB
