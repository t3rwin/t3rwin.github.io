The Compass Alliance Pathways

Tim ErwinSept, 2018

Overview

I built a portable mono audio amplifier to power a speaker salvaged from a TV. It is made from the LM386 amplfifier IC. The circuit is based off of example circuits from the IC's datasheet. I designed a PCB and assembled a portable speaker setup that can be used with any device with a 3.5 mm audio jack.

The audio amplifier can drive loads from 4Ω to 32Ω , and can be supplied with 5V to 12V. My circuit is currently wired to set the gain to 20, but there is a spot to install a resistor (R5) and a capacitor (C8) to adjust the gain to any value from 20 to 200.
