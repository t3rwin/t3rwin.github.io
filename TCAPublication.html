The Compass Alliance Pathways

Tim ErwinSept, 2018

Overview

The Compass Alliance is an organization, founded by 10 First Robotics Competition (FRC) teams, that provides resources and services to other FRC teams. One of these resources is "The Compass Alliance Pathways", which are documents written to guide less experienced teams through key aspects of becoming a successful team. In September 2018 when I was on FRC team Citrus Circuits, I wrote the "Electrical Systems and Components", and the "Pneumatic Systems" guides.
