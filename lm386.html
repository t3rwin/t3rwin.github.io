LM386 Audio Amplifier

Tim ErwinMay 20, 2020

Overview

I built a portable mono audio amplifier to power a speaker salvaged from a TV. The circuit is made from the LM386 amplfifier IC, and based off of example circuits from the datasheet. I designed a PCB and assembled a portable speaker setup that can be used with any device with a 3.5 mm audio jack.

The audio amplifier can drive loads from 4Ω to 32Ω , and can be powered by 5V to 12V. My circuit is currently wired to set the gain to 20, but the PCB includes spots for resistor R5 and capacitor C8 to adjust the gain to any value from 20 to 200. 

Software and Services:

  • Schematic and PCB design: KiCad
  • PCB Manufacturing: JLCPCB
